2. Backpropagation (Compute Gradients and Update Weights)

We’ll compute partial derivatives of the error/loss with respect to each weight and then do a simple gradient descent update:

w:=w−α∂Loss∂ww := w - \alpha \frac{\partial \text{Loss}}{\partial w}w:=w−α∂w∂Loss​

where α\alphaα is the learning rate (let’s pick α=0.1\alpha = 0.1α=0.1 for example).
